FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Purchase and device questions, without the fine print maze.

What happens after my 10 free games?
You can still open settings, records, the yaku collection, and achievements. Starting game 11 opens the Full Game purchase screen. Game 10 is never interrupted and its result remains available.
How do I restore my purchase?
Open Settings or the Full Game screen and choose Restore Purchases. Use the same Apple ID that made the purchase. Purchases are verified by Apple through StoreKit.
Does continuing a game use another free game?
No. A free game is counted only after a new legal game is created and saved. Returning to the same active game does not count it again.
Where are my records stored?
Game progress, settings, records, achievements, and yaku history are stored locally on your device. Tsumo Dragon does not require an account.
The game has no sound or haptics. What should I check?
Open Settings and confirm Music, Sound Effects, and Haptics are enabled. Also check your iPhone volume, Silent Mode, and any connected audio device.
Which devices and orientations are supported?
Tsumo Dragon is designed for iPhone and optimized for landscape play. Keep iOS updated for the best StoreKit, audio, and accessibility behavior.

Still need help?

Email us with your iPhone model, iOS version, app version, and a short description. Do not include payment card details or Apple ID passwords.
